Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply create an account, browse our courses, and click "Enroll Now" on any course you're interested in. You can start learning immediately after enrollment.

Are the courses aligned with school curriculum?

Yes, all our courses are aligned with NCERT, CBSE, ICSE, and major state board curricula to ensure they complement your school education.

Can I get help with my doubts?

Absolutely! We provide 24/7 doubt support through our expert teaching assistants. You can ask questions anytime and get detailed explanations.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, our mobile app is available for both Android and iOS. You can download it from Google Play Store or Apple App Store to learn on the go.
